Catch Me If You Can

Catch Me If You Can is a 2002 biographical crime film directed by Steven Spielberg, based on the life of Frank Abagnale. Before his 19th birthday, Frank successfully committed fraud by posing as a Pan Am pilot, doctor, and lawyer — all while being chased by the FBI.

Key Information

  • Director: Steven Spielberg
  • Release Year: 2002
  • Genre: Biography, Crime, Drama
  • Based On: The true story of Frank Abagnale Jr.

Main Characters

  • Frank Abagnale Jr.: A brilliant young con artist who forges checks and impersonates professionals across the country.
  • Carl Hanratty: A dedicated FBI agent determined to catch Frank and stop his crimes.
  • Frank Abagnale Sr.: Frank’s charismatic but struggling father, whose influence shapes his son's path.
