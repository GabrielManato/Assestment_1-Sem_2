Beef Kaldereta

Beef Kaldereta is a rich and savory Filipino stew made with tender beef chunks, liver spread, tomato sauce, and a medley of vegetables. It's perfect for special occasions or a comforting family dinner.

Ingredients

  • 1 kg beef chuck or stewing beef, cubed
  • 1 cup tomato sauce
  • 2 tablespoons liver spread
  • 2 medium potatoes, cubed
  • 2 medium carrots, sliced
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 green bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 cups beef broth or water
  • 2 tablespoons cooking oil
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce (optional)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

How to cook Beef Kaldereta

  1. Heat oil in a large pot. Sauté garlic and onion until fragrant.
  2. Add the beef cubes and cook until lightly browned on all sides.
  3. Pour in the tomato sauce and beef broth. Bring to a boil, then lower heat and simmer until the beef is tender (about 1 to 1.5 hours).
  4. Stir in the liver spread and mix well until fully dissolved.
  5. Add the potatoes and carrots. Cook until vegetables are tender.
  6. Add the bell peppers and soy sauce (if using). Simmer for another 5–7 minutes.
  7. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Serve hot with steamed rice.
